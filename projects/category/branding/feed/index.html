Project overview

VITABOWL founded with the aim of to inspire and empower people around the world to #keephealthy, with our clinically researched products.

We never compromise on quality. All our products are Banned Substance Free, Gluten-Free, GMO Free & manufactured in the INDIA to strict Goods Manufacturing Practice (GMP) standards. Furthermore, we provide Certificates of Analysis that validate the purity and level of active ingredients within a product.

Details

Year

2021

Services

Branding, Art Direction

Our Method

Experiencing years and years of aiding ethnic and minority groups, we understand their culture which is profoundly implied in the way they work, play and live.

Vitabowl’s mission focuses on enhancing lifestyle of people around the world, through sustainable , organic & healthy practices. The identity that we created captures the spirit of loving, connecting and sharing. We approached VItabowl in a modern way, to provide a graphic identity that would mark the brand on the market and could support it at all stages of its development in the long-haul. Therefore, we aware of how delightful and charming it is and aspire to share this unseen beauty to urban eyes.

An essential aspect of the renewal is ensuring that the visual identity is relevant in the present and future digital environment and can represent the institution on the international stage. Due to its diversity and variability, it can renew itself continuously, but it is still recognizable.

Project overview

Nature’s Crate has made sure that you get certified organic, natural products from our stores.

You can also purchase these products from the ease of your home. Your one stop destination for all things organic! From all Natural Toothbrush to Organic Groceries.

Details

Year

2022

Service

Packaging, Identity

Challenge

Our client is an organic and natural product seller who has experienced significant success and notable sales growth in the market. They approached us to create packaging for a new product in their existing dairy line – A2 Cow Ghee. Our challenge was to create packaging that would maintain the brand identity of this well-positioned company while also highlighting the acknowledged benefits of the brand such as freshness, quality, and taste through simplicity and whiteness. Essentially, we were tasked with associating the main character of the brand, the cow, with ghee and portraying it in a single shape.

Cow

To highlight the roots of the product

Aura

To indicate our moods & vibe

Flower

Indicates Eco-Friendly, Fresh Environment

Family

People who lives in the village

Sun

Represents Power and Joy

Happy Kid

Showcase benefits of the products & Tastes Good

Modern Village

Village with new equipment for better environment & needs

DNA

To indicate the health benefits of the product

Cycle

To keep the rural & the village environment

Our Method

A contemporary design was created with illustrations in minimal style, along with a smart use of brand elements/colours to achieve this.

The idea was to capture the story of Gir cow A2 ghee that is made in a traditional way, and render it in a fresh contemporary look. A touch of yellow was added to reflect the richness of the ghee. A special unit of cow was devised to depict the standard of the ghee.

Nature’s Crate introduces identity as a pact, an oath that honors its values and history. From the slender gesture of the iconic cow to the meticulousness or the proprietary Illustrations, from the textured backgrounds to the rough paint strokes, from the generous product visuals to the strong brand statements. the new brand assets have been tailor-made for the product.

Finally, the storytelling on pack highlights the strong messages and actions of the brand through the powerful design.
